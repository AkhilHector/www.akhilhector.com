Life at Zero | Lambasingi
By Akhil Pandey, December 09, 2015

In the process of exploring beautiful locations in and around Vizag I had an oppurtunity to visit Lambasingi which is a scenic magnifico located at 120-130 Kms from the city of Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh.
I wanted to have a raw essence of travel as a result me and my pack decided to visit this place on bike. It is pretty much a decent distance to visit on bike, it might nearly take 4 hours to commute from Visakhapatnam.

In addition to this there is one more location near Lambasingi called Paderu. In order to witness those pictures Do Check out.
